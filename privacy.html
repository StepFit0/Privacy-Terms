StepFit Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how StepFit (“the App”) handles your data. StepFit is designed and developed by Iman Mirzazadegan as a personal pedometer and fitness companion. The App is intentionally built to minimize the amount of personal data collected and to keep your information primarily on your own device.

1. Scope

This Policy applies to all versions of StepFit distributed through official app stores (such as Google Play) and covers all features available within the App, including step tracking, goals, history, widgets, notifications and Health Connect integration.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Step and activity data

StepFit uses your device’s motion sensors (e.g. step counter sensor) and/or Health Connect to read your daily step counts and related activity metrics. These values are:

  • stored locally on your device,
  • used to calculate statistics such as daily totals and history,
  • displayed in widgets and notifications for your convenience.

We do not send your step data to our own servers. All processing happens either on-device or through Health Connect, according to the permissions you grant.

2.2 Profile and configuration data

StepFit may optionally store profile information you provide, such as:

  • Name or nickname
  • Age, gender, height, weight
  • Preferred units (km / miles)
  • Daily step goal and reminder time

This information is used only to personalize your experience, for example:

  • calculating approximate distance or calories,
  • showing age‑appropriate statistics,
  • triggering reminders at the time you choose.

All of this data is stored locally using Android’s secure storage mechanisms (DataStore / Room) and is not transmitted to external servers by StepFit.

2.3 Health Connect data

If you connect StepFit to Health Connect, the App may read the data types that you explicitly allow, such as:

  • Steps
  • Distance
  • Total calories burned
  • Weight

StepFit uses this information only to display your activity inside the app and to keep your local statistics aligned with your existing health data. StepFit does not modify or delete external health records unless you explicitly enable write permissions for supported types (e.g. weight logs).

Health Connect itself is operated by Google and is subject to Google’s separate privacy and security policies.

3. Notifications and reminders

StepFit can schedule local notifications to remind you to move or to inform you when you reach your daily goal. These notifications are created and handled entirely on your device. No notification content is sent to external servers by the App.

4. Analytics and third‑party services

At the time of this version, StepFit does not embed third‑party advertising SDKs or behavior‑tracking analytics. If in the future lightweight crash reporting or anonymous usage metrics are introduced to improve stability and UX, this section of the Policy will be updated to describe exactly what is collected and how it is processed.

5. Data retention and deletion

Because StepFit stores data primarily on your device:

  • You may clear your history, reset today’s steps or modify your profile directly from the app settings.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all data stored by StepFit from your device.

If you use Health Connect, that platform may continue to hold your data according to its own retention policy. You can manage Health Connect records separately via the Health Connect app or system settings.

6. Security

StepFit relies on Android’s security model and storage mechanisms. While no mobile app can guarantee absolute security, StepFit:

  • does not transmit your step or profile data to its own servers,
  • limits permissions to what is necessary for pedometer and notification functionality,
  • uses well‑known libraries and APIs from the Android Jetpack ecosystem.

7. Children’s privacy

StepFit is not directed at children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child is using the App with personal information that should not be collected, you can remove that data by clearing the app settings or uninstalling the app on the relevant device.

8. Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect new functionality, legal requirements, or improvements in how data is handled. When significant changes are made, a new version of the Policy will be published together with the app update.
